About Green Grow Solar

Founded and led by master electrician Kelvin Bester, Green Grow Solar brings over 7 years of expertise in electrical and solar solutions. What began as a passion for sustainable energy has grown into a trusted name across the Helderberg region.

Our unique specialization in cannabis facility electrical systems sets us apart. We understand the specific requirements of cultivation facilities and provide tailored solutions that ensure optimal growth conditions while maintaining energy efficiency.

Our Mission

To provide premium solar and electrical solutions that enhance sustainability, reduce energy costs, and create reliable power systems for homes, businesses, and specialized facilities.
